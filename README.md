# 📊 Sales & Customer Dashboard (Tableau)

## 🔗 Project Overview

This project consists of two interactive dashboards built using **Tableau** to analyze **sales performance** and **customer behavior**.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into **actionable insights** that help businesses make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The dashboards answer key business questions:

* How is overall sales and profit performing over time?
* Which product subcategories drive the most revenue and profit?
* Are there any loss-making areas?
* Who are the top customers contributing to revenue?
* How is customer purchasing behavior distributed?

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Tableau Public** (Data Visualization & Dashboard Design)
* Data Cleaning & Preparation
* Calculated Fields
* Interactive Filters & Parameters

---

## 📊 Dashboards

### 📈 Sales Dashboard

Focuses on overall business performance:

* Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity (with YoY comparison)
* Sales & Profit trends over time
* Subcategory-wise performance (profit vs loss analysis)
* Identification of high-performing and underperforming segments

---

### 👥 Customer Dashboard

Focuses on customer behavior and value:

* Total Customers, Total Orders, and Average Revenue per Customer
* Customer distribution based on number of orders
* Top 10 customers by profit
* Identification of high-value customers

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales show an **overall upward trend** with seasonal fluctuations
* Certain subcategories generate **high sales but lower profit margins**
* A small group of customers contributes a **significant portion of total revenue**
* Most customers place **low-frequency orders**, while a few are high-value customers

---

## 📈 Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses:

* Identify top-performing products and optimize inventory
* Detect loss-making categories and improve profitability
* Focus on high-value customers for retention strategies
* Plan marketing and operations based on sales trends
